### ① Invalid key
> Thanks for reaching out! That error usually means the key was pasted with an extra space or only partially. Copy it straight from your purchase email (it starts with `mk-live-`), paste it into the MailFrame popup → Subscription, and hit Activate. If it still won't take, reply with the email you used at checkout and I'll re-issue your key.
>
> — MailFrame

### ② "I paid but don't know what to do"
> Welcome aboard, and thanks for subscribing! You're all set on our end. To start: (1) click the MailFrame icon in your browser toolbar, (2) open **Subscription** and paste the key from your purchase email, (3) you'll see "Activated." Then open any email in Gmail and press **S** for your first summary. Anything not working, just reply.
>
> — MailFrame

### ③ "It's slow / not summarizing"
> Sorry for the hassle! Two quick things: (1) reload MailFrame at `chrome://extensions`, then refresh your Gmail tab — that fixes most cases after an update. (2) Make sure you've opened a single thread and pressed **S**. The first summary after a quiet spell can take a few seconds while our service wakes up. If it's still stuck, tell me your plan and what you see and I'll dig in.
>
> — MailFrame

### ④ Cancel / refund

### ⑤ "Does it read all my emails?"
> Great question. No — MailFrame only processes the single thread you choose to summarize, at the moment you press **S** (or open an order to extract). It doesn't scan or store your inbox, and your **email content is never stored** — I keep only your subscription key and monthly usage counts.
>
> — MailFrame
